Why I Chose a Career Path of a Web Developer with Coding Temple

Becoming a Web Developer, Aug 24, 2022

There are a lot of reasons I would like to become a web developer. The first major reason is because I love to problem solve. Currently in my industry and profession I do alot of problem solving. The idea of being able to do a bunch of problem solving using my computer skills excites me in this field. It also excites me that I have the ability to be as creative as possible in this field of web development. With this class I am looking to potentially change my profession from a project manger in the construction field to move into either a program management role or even a developer role. Unlike most industries web development is something that is always evolving with new technology and you are able to be on the cutting edge of the newest technology. I chose Coding Temple after reading the great reviews from past alumni. All of them for the most part spoke very highly about this course. They also mentioned how the mentors are there to grow and work with you on any questions you have. Overall, I am very excited to start my new career path in web development using the building blocks I will learn from Coding Temple.
